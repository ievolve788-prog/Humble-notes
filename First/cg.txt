
### 1. Swaraj Party and Government Formation (1925-1930)

-  12 Dec 25  Swaraj party ki jeet & formation of new government
    
- Chunaav ke baad hi governor ne dr. Shivraj Munje ko government form karne ke liye invite kiya
    
- but Shivraj munje ne iss baat se mana kar diya.
    
- finally governor ne minority wali government banakar rule karna khud shuru kiya
    
- iss sarkaar ka tenure January 1930 se start hua.
    
- and is government dwara laye gaye bills & proposals ko assembly me khaarij kiya jane laga
    
- phir bhi kisi tarah ye sarkaar chalti rahi.
    

### 2. Swaraj Party Partition and E. Raghavendra Rao

- usi beech 1925 me CR Das ki mrityu ho jati hai. aur inki mrityu ke baad swaraaj party ka partition ho jata hai.
    
- E Raghavendra Rao jo swaraaj party se chunaav jeet kar aaye the woh ek nayi party banaye independent.
    
- iske baad 1926 me unhone fir chunaav lada aur nayi formed government me woh education minister banaye gaye.
    
- par agle hi saal 1928 me E raghavendra rao chief Minister jaise kaam karne lage.
    
- Baad me unhone wapas congress join karli swaraaj party chor kar.
    
- 1928 me congress ne CM nahi banaya, E ragh -avendra rao independent party ke bane CM the.
    

### 3. Jhanda Satyagrah 1923 (Flag Satyagraha)

-  Jhanda Satyagrah 1923 
    
- Jhanda styagrah Jhande ke apmaan ke virodh me tha.
    
- Present flag me jhande ke beech me chakra hai us time charkha hua karta tha. * is jhande ko freedom fighter apne symbolically use karte the jab british ne is flag ko insult kiya toh ye satyagrah phases me start hua ( 3 phases me )
    
- 1st phase → 18 March 1923 ko start hua.  Location : Jabalpur town hall
    
- 2nd phase → 31 March 1923  Location  → Bilaspur
    
- 3rd phase →1st April 1923  Location : Nagpur
    
- yahaan se ise poore india mein launch kiya.
    
- it means phase 3, Jhanda Satyagrah ka sabse bada event tha. Inme cg ke log shaamil hue the
    
- dhamtari tehsil se maximum log shaamil hue the.
    
- under the leadership of Pandit Sunderlal Sharma.
    
- kakinada me zyada log dhamtari se gaye ye  [Blank Space]  pandit Sunderlal Sharma ka bi leadership → Narayan rao Meghawale.

### 5. Civil Disobedience Movement (CDM) and Other Events

- Sunderlal Sharma yahin nahi ruke unhone aage bade karyakram kiye.
    
-  Janeu Karyakram Andolan 
    
    - during civil disobedience movement
        
    - (1922 or 1923) Me jab gandhiji ne Non cooperation movement wapas liya uske baad society me ek vaccum create ho gaya.
        
    - Zyadatar youth iske bad revolutionary activities karne lage.
        
    - isi ke baad gandhiji jail chale gaye lekin Later health issues ke kaaran woh waapas riha kar diye gaye.
        
- Bahar aate hi gandhiji ne apna doosra mass movement civil disobedience movement start kar diye.
    
- Iska impact poori country me dekhne mila.
    
- Dandi march se CDM ko gandhiji ne 12 March 1930 ko start kiya, 6 ko woh Sabarmati se Dandi pohonch gaye.
    
- aisa hi event hua Bilaspur me  Gajadhar [Saw/Rao]  ke leadership me hua jo  Thakur Pyarelal Singh  se inspired tha.
    
-  Bilaspur me Vaanar Sena ka gathan 
    
    - Vanar sena chote baccho ki sena thi.
        
    - Halanki chote bacchon ka aisa group Raipur me bana tha.
        
    - but vanar sena ka first formation  Vasudev Deoras  ne Bilaspur me kiya tha.
        
- Aage chalkar yati yatanlaal ne Raipur me ek chote bacche  Baliraam Aazaad  ki help se chote baccho ka group banwaya jo message convey karne ka kaam kiya karte the.
    
--  CDM: Bilaspur: Tiranga Jhanda 
    
     Bilaspur me rehne waale Kanti Kumaar Bharatiya ne Bilaspur ke ek government highschool building me August 1930 ko tiranga phehra diya jiske baad unhe arrest kar liya gaya.
        
- CDM start hone ke baad reforms ke liye London me  RTC (Round Table Conference)  hua.
    
    - RTC 1st me gandhiji  ne participate nahi kiya  toh irvin & gandhi ke beech ek pact sign hua.
        
    - jiske kaaran gandhiji  RTC 2  ko attend karne London gaye.
        
    - unke saath Chhattisgarh ke raja  Ramanuj Pratap Sinhdev  bhi gaye the.
        
    - Jis samay Ramanuj Pratap Sinhdev Round table conference attend kar rahe the us samay  koriya  me vidroh hua -  begaari pratha  ke kaaran  1932  mein.

    - wapas CDM ko  CDM 2.0  ke naam se chalu kiya.

### 5. 1935 Act and 1937 Election

-  1935 Act and Election 
    
- 1935 ke act ke baad  1937  ko chunav hua jisme  congress ko majority  mili.
    
- but Raghavendra Rao congress se chunaav nahi lad rahe the, ve  independent  chunav lad rahe the.
    
    - woh nirdaliya chunaav jeet gaye  bilaspur  se.
        
- 1937 ka chunav,  Pandit Ravishankar  ne  raipur  seat se nahi lada tha balki unhone  Saraipali  se jeel hasil ki.
    
- Raipur se  Thakur Pyarelal  jeete the.

- Iss chunav ke baad  Narayan Bhaskar Khare  ki sarkaar bani, jo leader bane.
    
    -  Pandit Ravi Shankar → Education Minister .
        
    -  Ghanshyam Singh Gupt → Speaker .
        
-  Note : Raghavendra Rao is mantrimandal me include nahi kiye gaye the, unhe  Leader of Opposition  banaya gaya tha.

-  Ravi Shankar Shukl  naye CM banaye gaye.
    
- Baad me unhone bhi  1939  me isteefa de diya.
